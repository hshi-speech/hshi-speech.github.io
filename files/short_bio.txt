Hao Shi (Member, IEEE) 
received the B.E. degree in computer science from Southwest Jiaotong University, Chengdu, China, in 2018, 
the M.S. degree in computer science from Tianjin University, Tianjin,China, in 2021, 
and the Ph.D. degree in informatics from Kyoto University, Kyoto, Japan, in 2024. 
From 2024 to 2025, he was a Researcher with Kyoto University. 
He is currently a Research Scientist with SB Intuitions.
His research interests include automatic speech recognition and speech enhancement.
